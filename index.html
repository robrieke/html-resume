About Robert

I'm a problem solver who craves as much scientific knowledge as possible. The ambition to learn and solve problems is my motivation for becoming an engineer with a broad knowledge base in every engineering discipline I can learn about. It has led to me earning four engineering degrees, including a Master's in Interdisciplinary Engineering from Purdue University and a Bachelor's in Nuclear Engineering Technology from Thomas Edison State University.

I have over a decade of engineering experience, which includes product designs/upgrades. I have experience with design and implementation of system upgrades for proton accelerator targets and a geotechnical centrifuge designed for use with explosive and radioactive materials.

I have used SolidWorks, Creo Parametric, and FreeCAD to create hundreds of equipment designs and drawing packages, and performed hundreds of design analyses including finite element, thermal, and flow.

I've enjoyed operating, maintaining, and troubleshooting a variety of systems across numerous engineering fields, including cryogenic, fluid (water/hydraulic/pneumatic), thermal, vacuum, electrical, electronic, centrifugal, and chemical/chemistry analysis. I've also had a blast working on 2.5D and lathe CNC's as well as manual machine shop and hand tools.

I have adopted project manager and operations manager responsibilities for multiple projects including detailed scheduling, collaboration with vendors and outside engineers, and risk management. My designs vary from simple parts to thermal testing chambers and hydrostatic testing systems, and I've proudly helped advance them from simple sketches through design and fabrication to release to end users.

I have used FMEA on new designs and failure analysis techniques such as Ishikawa and 5-Why when parts fail. I'm comfortable using a wide range of engineering-related programs and industrial microcontroller programs. I also have experience with at least eleven programming languages, and am in the process of increasing my experience with many of them.

On my off time I enjoy doing puzzles with family and working on my own personal equipment designs, which include a high-sensitivity seismometer and a home care robot.
